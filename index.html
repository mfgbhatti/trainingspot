Frequently Asked Questions

View the most frequently asked questions

  • How can I book and pay for the course?

    You book and pay using your credit card or debit card securely over the phone. You can also pay via bank transfer. Please contact us if you would like to make the payment via bank transfer.

  • What are the benefits of booking the course with Training Spot?

    We have helped people get licensed and find work.
    Benefits of booking your course with us:

    • Join a growing community of security professionals
    • Wide-choice of training providers, locations and course dates
    • Premium 24/7 Customer Experience
    • 0% Booking Commission
    • 100% Money-Back Training Guarantee
    • Instant Refund if the training course is cancelled
    • Access to CV Builder and Job Board to help you find work fast

  • When will I receive my examination result?

    Results are published online within just 5 working days after your examination date. You will be notified by SMS and email when they are ready to view - in the rare case your results are delayed, we will always keep you informed.

  • When will I receive my certificate?

    Certificates are usually dispatched 7 days after successful course completion. Please contact us via chat or phone - if it has been over 14 days so we can verify your address and arrange for a replacement. Please note, you don't need a certificate to apply for your SIA Licence.

  • Do I need my certificate to apply for SIA Licence?

    No, you do not need your certificate to apply for the SIA Licence. Your details are passed on to the SIA when you pass the course.

  • Q. What ID documents do I have to bring along?

    It is a regulatory requirement to do an identity check for all security-related qualifications. Therefore candidates who are attending Licence linked course are requested to provide the following upon arrival;

    • Two passport size photos
    • Original passport of any nationality or UK photo Drivers Licence
    • Two forms of address proof (originals)
    • Please see the joining instructions as a range of other documents can be accepted.

    You will not be able to sit the exam if you do not have the correct ID Documents. (Government Requirement)

  • I have not received the booking confirmation email?

    Let's investigate - first, let's check your SPAM folder, emails can occasionally land in there. You should have also received an SMS message containing joining instructions.
    If you don't find the email in your SPAM, we may have a wrong email address for you. Please contact us via chat or phone, so we can verify your email address and re-send you the booking confirmation email.

  • Does the course fee include the SIA Licence application fee?

    The course fee does not include the licence fee. The licence application fee of £210 is payable to directy to the SIA when applying for the licence - which is valid for three years.
